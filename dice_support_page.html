Support & Tips

Welcome to the support page for Dice by Phuti Cowrie! Here, you'll find helpful tips and information to get the most out of your dice-rolling experience.

How to Roll the Dice:

Whether you’re missing a dice during a game night or just prefer the convenience of digital dice, Dice by Phuti Cowrie has you covered. This app simulates the rolling of a standard six-sided dice with each number (1 through 6) having an equal probability of being rolled, ensuring fairness and unbiased results every time. Rolling the dice is as simple as a single tap on your screen. The app allows you to roll up to five dice simultaneously—just use the slider at the bottom of the screen to adjust the number of dice you want to roll.

Multiple Dice Themes:

Personalize your dice-rolling experience with a variety of visually appealing themes. Dice by Phuti Cowrie offers a selection of unique dice themes that can be easily switched to match your mood or the ambiance of your game. To change the theme, simply click on the sun icon (located to the left of the slider) and select your preferred theme. With themes ranging from classic styles to vibrant colors, there's something for everyone.

Changing Themes:

Switching between different dice themes is straightforward and quick. Tap the icon near the bottom of the screen, next to the slider, to bring up the theme options. Scroll through the available themes and select the one that best suits your style. Your chosen theme will instantly update, giving your dice a fresh new look.

Positioning the Dice:

The app also includes a drag-and-drop feature, allowing you to customize the positions of your dice on the screen. Simply tap and hold any die, then drag it to your desired location. This feature is especially useful when rolling multiple dice, making it easier to view results at a glance.
